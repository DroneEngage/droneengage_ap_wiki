============
Sound Plugin
============

The Sound Plugin enables audio capabilities for DroneEngage, including speaker output and microphone input for communication and alerts.

|

Features
========

- **Audio Alerts** - Play sounds for status notifications
- **Voice Communication** - Two-way audio between drone and operator
- **Speaker Output** - Play audio files or announcements from the drone

|

Hardware Requirements
=====================

- USB audio adapter or HAT with speaker/microphone support
- Compatible speakers and/or microphone

|

Configuration
=============

Configure audio devices in the plugin configuration file.

|
